Privacy Policy for Toonify AI

Toonify AI does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

When you upload a photo to apply an effect, it is processed temporarily using third-party AI services. The photo is deleted immediately after the transformation is complete.

We do not use cookies, tracking tools, or analytics. Your privacy is important to us.

Toonify AI does not require login or collect user-identifiable information.

If you have questions or concerns, contact us at support@clairpix.com.
